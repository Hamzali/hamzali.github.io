TARS

TARS is an industrial grade, ultra low-power, high-impact IoT platform which can read any kind of sensor at a distance through battery or regular power providing remote viewing, control and telemetry. It combines both hardware and software and its design allows easily pluggable sensors and batteries which leads to an easy deployment and setup.

TARS

Jan 1, 0001

TARS aims to provide optimal workflows within industries improving efficiency through utilizing latest technologies. It is an Industrial Internet-of-Things platform which consists of both hardware and software. The major developmental constraint of the product has been energy consumption followed by industrial robustness. Therefore, all the components and software have been designed accordingly. TARS modernizes the conventional processes facilitated by either human force or less optimal legacy systems, improves efficiency and lowers failure and error rates drastically, enables telemetry causing valuable data be stored on databases for further analysis, data mining and artificial intelligence applications, can serve under heavy workload or hazardous areas which cannot easily be handled or accessed by human force, provides a more flexible, affordable and easy-to-use solution compared to SCADA systems, presents an easy to deploy and mount hardware interface with pluggable sensors and batteries, offers secure and encrypted data transmission and interconnectedly can handle relatively complex problems acting as a swarm of devices.
